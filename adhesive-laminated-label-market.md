<p><p>The adhesive laminated label market is categorized primarily into two types: reels and sheets. Reels consist of continuous rolls of labels, ideal for high-speed automated labeling processes, commonly used in industries like food and beverage. Sheets, on the other hand, feature individual labels on flat sheets, suitable for manual application and smaller production runs. Both types offer unique advantages, catering to different labeling needs, from large-scale manufacturing to personalized labeling for crafts or small businesses.</p></p>
