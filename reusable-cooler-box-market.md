<p><p>The reusable cooler box market comprises various materials, each offering unique properties. PU foam provides excellent insulation, retaining cold temperatures efficiently. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is lightweight and cost-effective, ideal for budget-conscious consumers. Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) offers superior moisture resistance and durability, suitable for outdoor use. Expanded Polypropylene (EPP) is known for its flexibility and impact resistance, making it lightweight yet sturdy. Other materials may include alternatives like fiberglass or composite materials, catering to diverse consumer needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
