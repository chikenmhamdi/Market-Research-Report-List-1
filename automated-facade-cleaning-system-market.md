<p><strong>The Automated Facade Cleaning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact cleaning robot</li><li>Non-contact cleaning robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Facade Cleaning System market is divided into two primary types: contact cleaning robots and non-contact cleaning robots. Contact cleaning robots physically touch surfaces to remove dirt, employing brushes, squeegees, or pressure washing techniques. They are effective for tough grime and require regular maintenance. In contrast, non-contact cleaning robots utilize technologies such as ultrasonic waves, chemical sprays, or drones to clean facades without direct surface interaction, offering a gentler approach that minimizes potential damage and enhances safety in high-rise applications.</p></p>
