<p><p>The industrial fans and blowers market encompasses two key types: industrial fans and industrial blowers. Industrial fans are designed to move air in various applications, providing ventilation, cooling, and exhaust. They are further classified into axial and centrifugal fans based on their design and airflow direction. Industrial blowers, on the other hand, generate higher pressures and are used for applications like material handling, dust collection, and pneumatic conveying. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing operational efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
