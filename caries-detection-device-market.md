<p><p>The caries detection device market comprises various technologies, notably fluorescent and transillumination methods. Fluorescent technology leverages specific wavelengths of light to detect dental caries by highlighting areas of demineralization, enhancing visual inspection. Conversely, transillumination technology uses light to pass through the tooth structure, allowing for the identification of carious lesions based on changes in light transmission. Both technologies aim to improve early caries detection, promoting preventative care and better treatment outcomes in dental practice.</p></p>
