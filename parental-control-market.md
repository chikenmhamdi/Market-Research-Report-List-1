<p><p>The parental control market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based parental controls offer remote management and monitoring capabilities through online platforms, allowing parents to access features from any device with internet connectivity. In contrast, on-premises solutions are installed directly on a specific device, providing more localized control and privacy but limiting access to that device. Both types cater to different user needs, with cloud solutions emphasizing accessibility and on-premises focusing on security.</p></p>
