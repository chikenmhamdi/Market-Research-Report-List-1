<p><p>The air conditioning damper actuator market is segmented into three main types: pneumatic, electric, and others. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to control damper position, offering reliability in harsh environments. Electric actuators, powered by electrical signals, provide precise control and faster response times, making them popular in modern HVAC systems. The "others" category includes hybrid or manual actuators, which cater to niche applications where specific control mechanisms or functionalities are required. Each type serves distinct operational needs in HVAC systems.</p></p>
