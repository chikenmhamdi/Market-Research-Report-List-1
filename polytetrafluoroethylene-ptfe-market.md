<p><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is a high-performance fluoropolymer known for its exceptional chemical resistance and thermal stability. In the market, PTFE is typically categorized into two main types: particle PTFE and fine powder PTFE. Particle PTFE consists of larger granules used in applications requiring durability and impact resistance, while fine powder PTFE has a smaller particle size, enhancing its flow properties and making it ideal for applications like coatings and seals where precise formulations are necessary. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
