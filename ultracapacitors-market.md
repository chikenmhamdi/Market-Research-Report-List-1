<p><ul><li>Double Layer Capacitance</li><li>Faraday Capacity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultracapacitor market is primarily categorized into two types: double layer capacitance and Faraday capacitance. Double layer capacitors utilize electrostatic charge accumulation in an electric double layer at the interface of the electrode and electrolyte, offering high power density and rapid charging. In contrast, Faraday capacitance involves electrochemical processes where charge is stored through reversible redox reactions, providing higher energy density. Together, these technologies serve diverse applications, enhancing energy storage solutions in electric vehicles, renewable energy, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
