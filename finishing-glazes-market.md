<p><p>The finishing glazes market comprises two primary types: solid and liquid glazes. Solid glazes are typically available in powder form, requiring mixing with water or a medium before application. They offer durability and are favored for their versatility in various artistic and industrial applications. Liquid glazes, on the other hand, come ready to use, providing a convenient option for users. They offer smoother application and quicker drying times, making them popular in ceramics and other crafts for achieving vibrant finishes.</p></p>
