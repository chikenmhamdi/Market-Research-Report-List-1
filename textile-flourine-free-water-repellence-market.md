<p><strong>The Textile Flourine-free Water Repellence Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weak Cationic</li><li>Weakly Anionic</li><li>Non-ionic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile fluorine-free water repellence market features three primary types: weak cationic, weakly anionic, and non-ionic treatments. Weak cationic agents are positively charged and enhance water resistance on negatively charged textile fibers, providing good durability. Weakly anionic agents possess a negative charge and are compatible with various substrates, offering moderate performance. Non-ionic treatments, lacking charge, are versatile and often used for delicate fabrics, ensuring water repellence without altering the fabric's properties dramatically. Each type caters to specific textile requirements and applications.</p></p>
