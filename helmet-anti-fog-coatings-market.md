<p><ul><li>Hydrophilic Coating</li><li>Nanocoating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helmet anti-fog coatings market includes two primary types: hydrophilic coatings and nanocoatings. Hydrophilic coatings work by attracting water molecules, spreading them into a thin, uniform film that prevents fogging on the surface. This ensures optimal visibility in various conditions. Nanocoatings, on the other hand, utilize nanotechnology to create a protective layer that repels water and enhances durability, offering superior resistance to fogging and environmental wear. Both types enhance user safety and comfort in demanding environments.</p></p>
