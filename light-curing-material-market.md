<p><ul><li>External Emulsification Type</li><li>Non-ionic Self-emulsifying Type</li><li>Ion-based Self-emulsifying Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light curing material market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **External Emulsification Type**: This type involves the use of external agents to stabilize emulsions, enhancing the curing process through light exposure.</p><p>2. **Non-ionic Self-emulsifying Type**: These materials facilitate self-emulsification without ion charges, promoting uniform dispersion and improved light penetration during curing.</p><p>3. **Ion-based Self-emulsifying Type**: Utilizing ionic agents, this type enhances emulsification stability and curing efficiency through targeted interactions with light, optimizing the overall performance of light curing applications.</p></p>
