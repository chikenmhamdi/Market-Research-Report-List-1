<p><strong>The Vapor Recovery Units (VRU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensation</li><li>Adsorption with Carbon Beds or Molecular Sieve</li><li>Absorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vapor Recovery Units (VRUs) are systems designed to capture and reclaim vapors released during the storage and transfer of volatile organic compounds. The market can be classified into three main types: </p><p>1. **Condensation** - where vapors are cooled to condense liquid.</p><p>2. **Adsorption** - utilizing carbon beds or molecular sieves to capture vapors through surface adhesion.</p><p>3. **Absorption** - involving a solvent that dissolves vapors, allowing for recovery. Each method offers distinct benefits based on operational needs and environmental regulations.</p></p>
