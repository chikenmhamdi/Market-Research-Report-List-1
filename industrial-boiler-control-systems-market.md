<p><strong>The Industrial Boiler Control Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modulating Control</li><li>On/Off Control</li><li>High-fire/Low-fire Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial boiler control systems encompass various types that manage boiler operations efficiently. Modulating control adjusts the fuel and air supply based on real-time demand, enhancing energy efficiency. On/Off control operates the boiler in a binary mode, either fully on or off, leading to simpler operation but less efficiency. High-fire/Low-fire control features two operational modes, allowing the boiler to run at high or low capacities, providing flexibility for varying load conditions while maintaining efficiency and response time.</p></p>
