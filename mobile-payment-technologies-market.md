<p><p>Mobile payment technologies encompass two primary market types: proximity payments and remote payments. Proximity payments involve transactions conducted in-person using near-field communication (NFC) or QR codes, facilitating quick and secure exchanges at point-of-sale terminals. Conversely, remote payments enable users to make purchases via mobile devices over the internet, such as app-based transactions or online shopping. Both types enhance convenience and speed in financial transactions, catering to diverse consumer preferences in a growing digital economy.</p></p>
