<p><p>Fiber Reinforced Plastics (FRP) panels are categorized based on the type of reinforcing fibers used. Glass fibers are the most common, offering good strength and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for construction and automotive applications. Carbon fibers provide high strength-to-weight ratio and stiffness, ideal for aerospace and high-performance sports equipment. Aramid fibers, known for their toughness and thermal resistance, are used in protective gear and military applications. Other fibers may include natural or synthetic alternatives, catering to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
