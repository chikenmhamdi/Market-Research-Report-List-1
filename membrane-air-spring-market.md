<p><strong>The Membrane Air Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Sealing</li><li>Non Self-sealing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The membrane air spring market is divided into two primary types: self-sealing and non-self-sealing. Self-sealing air springs automatically maintain pressure by using a sealing mechanism that prevents air loss, enhancing reliability and reducing maintenance requirements. Non-self-sealing air springs, on the other hand, require manual monitoring and inflation as they do not retain air without external intervention. These distinctions significantly impact applications across industries, with self-sealing springs being favored for their ease of use and efficiency.</p></p>
