<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint plasticizers market is categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based plasticizers are integrated into waterborne paints, offering low volatile organic compounds (VOCs), ease of application, and reduced environmental impact. Solvent-based plasticizers, on the other hand, are used in traditional oil-based paints, providing enhanced flexibility and durability but often releasing higher VOCs. Each type serves distinct applications, with water-based options increasingly favored for eco-friendly initiatives, while solvent-based products remain popular for their performance in specific industrial uses.</p></p>
