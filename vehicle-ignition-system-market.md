<p><p>The vehicle ignition system market primarily comprises two key components: spark plugs and ignition coils. Spark plugs ignite the air-fuel mixture in internal combustion engines, ensuring efficient combustion and optimal engine performance. Ignition coils, on the other hand, transform battery voltage into a high voltage necessary to create a spark at the spark plugs. Both components are essential for starting the engine and maintaining its operation, contributing significantly to the overall performance, efficiency, and emissions of the vehicle.</p></p>
