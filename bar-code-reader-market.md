<p><p>The barcode reader market encompasses two main types: handheld and stationary barcode scanners. Handheld barcode scanners are portable devices that allow users to scan barcodes quickly and conveniently in various settings, such as retail and warehouses. In contrast, stationary barcode scanners are fixed devices typically used at checkout counters or conveyor belts, automatically reading barcodes as items pass through. Both types enhance inventory management and sales processes by improving accuracy and efficiency in data capture and tracking.</p></p>
