<p><p>The skid steer market comprises two main types: wheeled and tracked. Wheeled skid steers feature rubber tires, enhancing speed and maneuverability on smooth surfaces, making them ideal for urban environments and material handling. In contrast, tracked skid steers utilize rubber or steel tracks, providing superior stability and traction on uneven terrain, making them suitable for construction and landscaping in challenging conditions. Both types are versatile, compact machines used for various applications, including digging, lifting, and grading.</p></p>
