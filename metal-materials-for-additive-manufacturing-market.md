<p><p>Metal materials for additive manufacturing encompass various techniques, primarily Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to fuse metal powders layer by layer, suitable for complex geometries and high-density parts. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powder, ideal for titanium and superalloys, enabling rapid production. Other methods include binder jetting and directed energy deposition, which cater to diverse applications by leveraging different materials and manufacturing capabilities.</p></p>
