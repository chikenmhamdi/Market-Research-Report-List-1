<p><p>The explosive trace detection market encompasses various technologies designed to identify trace amounts of explosives. Chemiluminescence relies on chemical reactions that emit light to detect explosives. Thermo-redox techniques analyze heat changes during reactions involving explosives. Amplifying fluorescent polymer methods use luminescent polymers to enhance detection sensitivity. Mass spectrometry provides precise molecular identification by measuring mass-to-charge ratios, while ion mobility spectrometry separates ions based on their movement through a gas, offering rapid and sensitive detection of explosive traces.</p></p>
