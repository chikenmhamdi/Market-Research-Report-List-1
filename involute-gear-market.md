<p><p>The involute gear market comprises several types, including face gears, spur gears, space gears, and other variations. Face gears feature a face that meshes with a pinion at a right angle, enabling unique power transmission applications. Spur gears are the most common, characterized by parallel teeth and efficient power transfer. Space gears, designed for three-dimensional applications, enhance mobility in compact systems. Other market types may include helical and bevel gears, each serving specific mechanical needs across various industries.</p></p>
