<p><strong>The Crystal Growing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crystal growing system market features key types such as the Czochralski (CZ) method furnace and the Floating Zone (FZ) method furnace. The CZ method involves melting a material and slowly pulling a seed crystal upwards to form large single crystals, widely used for semiconductor substrates. In contrast, the FZ method utilizes a localized heat zone to melt a rod of material, allowing for control over impurities, making it ideal for high-purity crystal production. Both methods cater to various industrial applications.</p></p>
