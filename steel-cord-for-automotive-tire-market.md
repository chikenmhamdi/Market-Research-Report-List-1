<p><p>The automotive tire market features various types of steel cords, classified by tensile strength. Normal Tensile (NT) cords offer basic strength and are typically used in standard tires. High Tensile (HT) cords provide enhanced durability for performance tires. Super Tensile (ST) cords deliver superior strength, improving tire longevity and safety under demanding conditions. Ultra Tensile (UT) cords represent the highest strength, designed for high-performance and specialty tires, ensuring optimal handling and resistance to wear. Each type addresses specific tire requirements in the automotive sector.</p></p>
