<p><p>The Education Learning Management System (LMS) market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based LMS offers flexibility and accessibility, allowing users to access educational content from anywhere with an internet connection. It typically features automatic updates and lower upfront costs. Conversely, on-premises LMS requires installation on local servers, providing greater control over data and security but often involves higher initial costs and maintenance responsibilities. Each type serves different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
