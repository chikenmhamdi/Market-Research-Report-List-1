<p><p>Corrosion Under Insulation (CUI) coatings are specialized protective coatings designed to prevent corrosion on metal surfaces that are insulated. Commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation, these coatings help mitigate the risks associated with moisture accumulation and thermal cycling, which can lead to localized corrosion. CUI coatings provide a barrier against moisture and environmental contaminants, extending the lifespan of insulated pipelines and equipment.</p><p>The CUI coatings market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing investments in industrial infrastructure and the need for maintenance to prevent costly equipment failures. Furthermore, growing awareness of the detrimental impacts of corrosion on safety and operational efficiency is propelling demand. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.3% during the forecast period. </p><p>Latest trends show advancements in coating technologies, including the development of high-performance, eco-friendly formulations that enhance durability while reducing environmental impact.
