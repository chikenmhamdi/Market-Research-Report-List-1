<p><p>Injection Grade Polypropylene Oxide Elastomers are categorized into two main types: Mechanical Blend Type and Chemical Graft Type. The Mechanical Blend Type involves physical mixing of polypropylene oxide with other polymers, providing enhanced elasticity and processability. In contrast, Chemical Graft Type Elastomers are created through chemical reactions that bond polypropylene oxide with different polymer chains, resulting in improved compatibility and adhesion properties. Both types cater to various applications in automotive, construction, and consumer goods due to their versatile performance characteristics.</p></p>
