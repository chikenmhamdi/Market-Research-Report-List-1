<p><p>The Online Admissions Software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software operates on remote servers, offering scalable, flexible access, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs, making it ideal for institutions with varying needs. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on an institution's servers, providing greater control over data and customization options, but typically requires higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different institutional preferences and operational requirements.</p></p>
