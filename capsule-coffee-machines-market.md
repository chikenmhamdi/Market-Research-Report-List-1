<p><ul><li>Closed Source System</li><li>Open Source System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capsule coffee machines come in two primary market types: closed source systems and open source systems. Closed source systems operate with proprietary capsules designed specifically for that machine, limiting consumer choice but ensuring consistent quality and taste. In contrast, open source systems allow users to utilize third-party capsules or refillable options, providing greater flexibility and cost-effectiveness. This distinction influences consumer preferences, with some opting for the convenience of closed systems while others prefer the versatility of open systems for brewing their coffee.</p></p>
