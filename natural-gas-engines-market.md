<p><strong>The Natural Gas Engines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External-mix Natural Gas Engine</li><li>Internal-mix Natural Gas Engine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural gas engines market is segmented into external-mix and internal-mix types. External-mix natural gas engines utilize a separate air and gas mixing system, allowing for precise control over the mixture before combustion, leading to improved efficiency and lower emissions. In contrast, internal-mix natural gas engines mix air and gas within the engine itself, simplifying design but potentially affecting performance. Both types cater to diverse applications, including transportation, propulsion, and stationary power generation, addressing the growing demand for cleaner energy solutions.</p></p>
