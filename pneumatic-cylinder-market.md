<p><p>The pneumatic cylinder market is primarily categorized into three types: single-acting cylinders, double-acting cylinders, and other types. Single-acting cylinders utilize air pressure to move the piston in one direction, relying on external forces for return. In contrast, double-acting cylinders operate using compressed air for both extending and retracting actions, offering greater force and control. Other types include specialized designs like rodless or guided cylinders, catering to specific applications and enhancing automation efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
