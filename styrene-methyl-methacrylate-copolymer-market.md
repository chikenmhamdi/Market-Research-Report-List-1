<p><p>The Styrene Methyl Methacrylate Copolymer market can be categorized based on production methods, primarily the Isobutylene and Ethylene methods. The Isobutylene method involves polymerizing styrene and methyl methacrylate in the presence of isobutylene, leading to enhanced flexibility and chemical resistance. In contrast, the Ethylene method utilizes ethylene in the polymerization process, resulting in different mechanical properties and stability. Each method influences the copolymer's attributes, making them suitable for various applications in coatings, adhesives, and plastics.</p></p>
