<p><p>The Lime Desiccant market is categorized into two main types: physical and chemical desiccants. Physical desiccants, such as calcium oxide, absorb moisture primarily through adsorption processes, making them effective in maintaining low humidity levels. In contrast, chemical desiccants involve specific chemical reactions to remove moisture, often providing higher moisture absorption capacity. Both types are utilized across various industries, including packaging, electronics, and pharmaceuticals, to protect products from moisture-related damage during storage and transportation.</p></p>
