<p><p>The reactor mechanical seals market can be categorized into three main types: single end, double-sided, and multi-faceted seals. Single end seals are designed for applications with one sealing surface, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Double-sided seals feature two sealing surfaces, enhancing reliability for high-pressure and high-temperature environments. Multi-faceted seals offer a combination of various sealing geometries, providing optimal flexibility and performance in complex applications. Each type serves specific operational needs, ensuring efficient sealing in industrial reactors.</p></p>
