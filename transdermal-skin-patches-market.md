<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Transdermal Skin Patches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drug in Adhesive</li><li>Matrix</li><li>Reservoir</li><li>Vapor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transdermal skin patches are categorized based on their design and drug delivery mechanisms. Drug-in-adhesive patches incorporate the drug into the adhesive layer, providing a simple and effective delivery. Matrix patches consist of a drug-embedded matrix allowing controlled release. Reservoir patches feature a separate drug reservoir with a rate-controlling membrane, ensuring precise dosing. Vapor patches utilize volatile substances that diffuse through the skin as vapor, while other types may include combination designs or specialized formulations to enhance efficacy and user experience.</p></p>
