<p><p>The Polypropylene (PP) resin market is segmented into four main types: Homopolymer, High Crystalline, Block Copolymer, and Random Copolymer. Homopolymer PP offers high stiffness and strength, making it suitable for rigid applications. High Crystalline PP has a higher melting point and enhanced thermal properties. Block Copolymer exhibits improved impact resistance and elasticity due to its structured design. Random Copolymer combines different monomers for better clarity and softness, making it ideal for packaging and consumer goods. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, driving market growth.</p></p>
