<p><p>Plastic welding machines, or plastic welders, are categorized into three main types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual welders. Automatic welders utilize advanced technology for fully automated processes, enhancing efficiency and precision in large-scale production. Semi-automatic welders strike a balance, offering some automation while requiring operator input for specific tasks. Manual welders are operated entirely by hand, providing flexibility for smaller, intricate jobs but demanding more skill. Each type caters to different production needs and operational requirements in the plastic welding market.</p></p>
