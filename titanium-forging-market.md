<p><strong>The Titanium Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impression Die</li><li>Open Die</li><li>Rolled Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Titanium Forging Market includes three primary types: Impression Die, Open Die, and Rolled Ring. Impression Die forging shapes titanium into precise, complex forms using dies, ideal for high-strength aerospace and automotive parts. Open Die forging involves hammering or pressing titanium between flat dies, suitable for large, simple components like shafts. Rolled Ring forging forms seamless rings by rolling a pierced billet, commonly used in bearings, flanges, and turbines, offering high structural integrity and performance in critical applications.</p></p>
