<p><p>The Mobile Peer-To-Peer (P2P) Money Transfer market comprises two main types: remote payment and proximity payment. Remote payment allows users to send money over the internet, often via apps, enabling transactions from any location. This is ideal for long-distance transfers. In contrast, proximity payment facilitates transactions between users who are physically close, typically using technologies like NFC or QR codes, making it suitable for in-person exchanges. Both types enhance convenience and speed in money transfers.</p></p>
