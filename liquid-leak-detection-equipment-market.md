<p><p>The Liquid Leak Detection Equipment market is categorized into two main types: invasive and non-invasive leak detection. Invasive leak detection involves physically penetrating the infrastructure or environment to monitor for leaks, often providing direct access to the source for accurate measurements. Conversely, non-invasive leak detection employs sensor technology and advanced monitoring techniques that assess the surroundings without direct contact, allowing for leak detection without disrupting the system. Both methods are crucial for preventing damage and ensuring safety in various applications.</p></p>
