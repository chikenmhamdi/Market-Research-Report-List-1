<p><p>Dental articulating paper is critical for assessing occlusion in dentistry. The market is divided into two primary types: horseshoe and straight strip. Horseshoe articulating paper features a curved design that allows for broader coverage of dental arch, facilitating easier manipulation during examinations. In contrast, straight strip articulating paper offers a linear format, making it ideal for targeted occlusal assessments in specific areas. Both types play essential roles in diagnosing bite issues and ensuring proper dental alignments.</p></p>
