<p><strong>The Optical Circulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode Type</li><li>Polarization-Maintaining Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical circulator market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-Mode and Polarization-Maintaining types. Single-Mode optical circulators are designed to facilitate light propagation in a single pathway, ideal for long-distance communication, while ensuring minimal signal loss. Polarization-Maintaining optical circulators, on the other hand, preserve the polarization state of light, making them essential for applications requiring high sensitivity and stability, such as fiber optic sensors and advanced telecommunications. Both types serve critical functions in modern fiber optic networks.</p></p>
