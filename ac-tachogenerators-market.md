<p><strong>The AC Tachogenerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Induction Tachogenerators</li><li>Synchronous Tachogenerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC tachogenerator market features primarily two types: induction tachogenerators and synchronous tachogenerators. Induction tachogenerators operate based on electromagnetic induction, providing reliable speed feedback in various applications, particularly in low-speed environments. In contrast, synchronous tachogenerators use a rotating magnetic field synchronized with the rotor, offering high precision and stability in speed measurement, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Both types serve essential roles in automation, control systems, and electric drives across diverse industries.</p></p>
