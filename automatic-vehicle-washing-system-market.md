<p><ul><li>Tunnel Type Automatic Vehicle Washing System</li><li>Rollovers Type Automatic Vehicle Washing System</li><li>Touchless In-Bay Automatic Vehicle Washing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic vehicle washing system market comprises three main types: Tunnel Type, Rollover Type, and Touchless In-Bay systems. Tunnel Type systems utilize a conveyor to move vehicles through a series of cleaning mechanisms, ensuring thorough washing. Rollover Type systems involve a stationary vehicle while the cleaning equipment moves over it, providing an efficient wash. Touchless In-Bay systems rely on high-pressure water jets and specialized chemicals to clean without physical contact, minimizing the risk of scratches or damage while optimizing convenience.</p></p>
