<p><p>Foam board insulation is a popular material used to enhance energy efficiency in buildings. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) foam board is lightweight, cost-effective, and offers good thermal resistance. XPS (Extruded Polystyrene) foam board is denser, moisture-resistant, and provides higher compressive strength, making it ideal for below-grade applications. Polyurethane foam board offers superior insulation with a high R-value per inch, excellent moisture resistance, and durability, often used in demanding environments. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing cost, performance, and application requirements.</p></p>
