<p><p>The biomedical optical fiber sensor market includes various types of sensors, notably fiber optic temperature sensors and fiber optic pressure sensors. Fiber optic temperature sensors utilize the principle of light transmission through fibers to accurately measure temperature changes in biological environments, offering high sensitivity and immunity to electromagnetic interference. Fiber optic pressure sensors, on the other hand, detect pressure variations by analyzing light changes within the fiber. Both sensor types are essential for non-invasive monitoring in medical applications, enhancing diagnostic and therapeutic capabilities.</p></p>
