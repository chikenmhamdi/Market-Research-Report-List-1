<p><p>The narrow linewidth tunable laser market encompasses several types, including semiconductor lasers, which are compact and efficient for applications like telecommunications. Distributed Bragg reflector lasers (DBR lasers) utilize refractive index modulation to achieve tunability and narrow linewidth, suitable for spectroscopy.
