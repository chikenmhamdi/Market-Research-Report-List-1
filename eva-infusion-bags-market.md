<p><ul><li>Single Chamber</li><li>Multi Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EVA infusion bags are categorized into two primary types: single chamber and multi-chamber bags. Single chamber bags contain a single type of solution, ensuring straightforward administration of medications or nutrients. In contrast, multi-chamber bags separate different components within a single container, allowing for greater flexibility in mixing medications or nutrients just before administration. This separation helps maintain the stability of each component, reducing waste and enhancing patient safety during intravenous therapy. Both types cater to various medical needs and preferences.</p></p>
