<p><p>The Anti-Seismic Support Systems market comprises various support types designed to enhance structural resilience during seismic events. Cylindrical supports are typically used for vertical loads, allowing for flexibility and stability. Bushing supports incorporate rubber or polymer elements to absorb shocks and vibrations, effectively reducing stress on structures. Conical supports offer a tapered design that improves load distribution and lateral stability, making them suitable for diverse architectural applications. Together, these systems play a crucial role in safeguarding structures against seismic forces.</p></p>
