<p><p>The artificial colors market can be segmented into powder and granule types. Powdered artificial colors are fine particles that provide intense pigmentation and are widely used in food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals for their easy dispersion and vibrant hues. On the other hand, granule artificial colors are larger, more uniform particles that dissolve more slowly, making them suitable for specific applications where controlled release of color is desired. Both types serve diverse industries, catering to varying formulations and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
