<p><ul><li>Solid State LiDAR</li><li>Mechanical LiDAR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Mounted LiDAR market is primarily divided into two types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR utilizes fixed optics and electronic components to capture data, offering durability and reduced moving parts, ideal for compact designs and cost efficiency. Mechanical LiDAR, in contrast, employs rotating components to scan the environment, providing high-resolution and long-range data, making it suitable for complex mapping and detailed environment perception. Both types cater to various applications in autonomous vehicles and mapping technologies.</p></p>
