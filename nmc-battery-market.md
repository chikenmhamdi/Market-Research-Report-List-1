<p><p>NMC batteries, utilizing nickel, manganese, and cobalt, come in three primary types: prismatic, soft pack, and cylindrical. Prismatic batteries are flat and rectangular, offering higher energy density and compact designs, ideal for electric vehicles and stationary storage. Soft pack batteries feature flexible, pouch-like structures, allowing lightweight and space-efficient designs, often used in portable electronics. Cylindrical batteries, with a round shape, are robust, widely used in various applications from electric vehicles to power tools, known for their reliability and ease of manufacturing.</p></p>
