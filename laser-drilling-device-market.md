<p><p>The laser drilling device market comprises several types, primarily focusing on YAG, fiber, and CO2 laser technologies. YAG laser drilling devices utilize neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet crystals, offering high precision for hard materials. Fiber laser drilling devices, known for their efficiency and versatility, utilize fiber optics to deliver focused energy, making them suitable for various applications. CO2 laser drilling devices, employing carbon dioxide gas, are effective for non-metal materials, providing large-area processing and high-speed capabilities in industrial settings.</p></p>
