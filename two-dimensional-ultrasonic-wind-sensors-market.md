<p><p>Two-Dimensional Ultrasonic Wind Sensors utilize ultrasonic signals to measure wind speed and direction. In the two-path configuration, the device employs two ultrasonic paths to determine wind components in two dimensions, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution for basic meteorological needs. The three-path configuration enhances accuracy by using three ultrasonic paths to provide more robust data on wind velocity and direction, enabling sophisticated analysis suitable for advanced atmospheric studies and applications in industries such as aviation and meteorology.</p></p>
