<p><ul><li>Differential Measurement Amplifier</li><li>Noise Measurement Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The measurement amplifiers market consists of various types, including differential measurement amplifiers and noise measurement amplifiers. Differential measurement amplifiers are designed to amplify the difference between two input signals while rejecting common-mode noise, making them ideal for precise applications in instrumentation and data acquisition. Noise measurement amplifiers, on the other hand, focus on amplifying weak electrical signals while minimizing the impact of noise, providing valuable insights in fields like telecommunications and audio engineering. Both play crucial roles in accurate signal processing.</p></p>
