<p><p>The hair color market consists of three main types: permanent, semi-permanent, and temporary hair dyes. </p><p>Permanent hair dye provides long-lasting results by penetrating the hair shaft, effectively covering gray hair. Semi-permanent dye offers a temporary solution that lasts several weeks, gradually fading with washes; it's ideal for enhancing natural color. Temporary hair dye is the most short-lived, providing vibrant color for a single wash or a few days, appealing for those seeking a quick change without commitment. Each type caters to varying consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
