<p><ul><li>On premise ERP</li><li>Cloud-based ERP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ERP systems are categorized into two main market types: on-premise ERP and cloud-based ERP. On-premise ERP involves software installed locally on a company's servers, offering complete control and customization, but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance efforts. In contrast, cloud-based ERP solutions are hosted off-site by providers, offering scalability, lower initial costs, and easier updates. Businesses often choose between these models based on factors like budget, scalability needs, and IT infrastructure capabilities.</p></p>
