<p><p>Email marketing software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing for easy access, scalability, and reduced maintenance, making it ideal for businesses of all sizes. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering more control over data and customization, but requiring greater upfront investment and ongoing management. Each type serves different business needs based on preferences for accessibility, control, and resource allocation.</p></p>
