<p><p>The polyethylene tape market comprises various types, including cold applied coating adhesive tape, polyethylene adhesive tape, and low-density polyethylene film tape. Cold applied coating adhesive tape is designed for easy application in low-temperature conditions, providing excellent insulation and protection. Polyethylene adhesive tape offers strong adhesion and waterproof qualities, suitable for various sealing and packaging applications. Low-density polyethylene film tape is lightweight, flexible, and resistant to moisture, making it ideal for bundling, packaging, and protecting surfaces during storage or transport.</p></p>
